Kips Tourism

Last Updated: 17 November 2025

At Kips Tourism, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data when you use our website, make bookings, or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you interact with Kips Tourism:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, address, travel preferences, passport/ID details (if needed), date of birth.
  • Booking Details: Travel dates, destinations, number of travelers, hotel preferences, transport requirements.
  • Payment Information: Card or payment details (securely processed via payment gateways).
  • Communication Data: Messages, requests, emails, and other communications you send us.
  • Technical / Non-Personal Data: IP address, device/browser details, cookies, website usage data, and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized travel quotes and itineraries.
  • Contact you to discuss plans, confirm bookings, and send updates.
  • Process bookings and manage travel arrangements.
  • Send promotional offers (only if opted in).
  • Improve website and service experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Travel partners (airlines, hotels, local guides)
  • Payment processors
  • Legal authorities when required
  • Service providers (analytics, security, support)

4. Data Security

We use SSL encryption, secure servers, restricted data access, and continuous monitoring to protect your data. However, no system is fully secure.

5. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of data (with restrictions)
  • Opt-out from marketing emails

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance experiences and analyze trends. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Third-Party Links

We are not responsible for external websites linked on our site.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will always be posted here.
