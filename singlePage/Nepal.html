Nepal Tours

5 Nights & 6 Days Ex. Kathmandu

Day 01: Tribhuvan International Airport – Nagarkot

After completion of immigration formalities at the Kathmandu Airport exit door, you will be met by our representative and transferred to Nagarkot, a scenic hill station. Overnight at the hotel in Nagarkot.

Day 02: Nagarkot – Pokhara

After breakfast, transfer to Pokhara, a picturesque destination famous for its lakes and Himalayan views. En route, visit Bhaktapur, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Overnight stay at the hotel in Pokhara.

Day 03: Pokhara Sightseeing

Early morning visit to Sarangkot for sunrise and Himalayan views. Later, explore Vindyavasini Temple, David's Fall, and Gupteshwar Mahadev Caves. Evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Pokhara.

Day 04: Pokhara - Kathmandu

Return to Kathmandu. En route, visit Manakamana Temple via cable car (on direct payment basis). Evening free for shopping or personal activities. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.

Day 05: Kathmandu Sightseeing

After breakfast, visit Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, Kathmandu Durbar Square, and Swayambhunath Stupa (Monkey Temple). Evening free for leisure. Overnight at the hotel in Kathmandu.

Day 06: Hotel - Tribhuvan International Airport

After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your return journey.

  • English Speaking Driver Cum Guide
  • Entrance fee to all sites based on itinerary
  • Airport Pick up & Drop

6 Nights & 7 Days Ex. Kathmandu

Day 01: Tribhuvan International Airport – Hotel – Kathmandu Sightseeing

After completion of immigration formalities at the Kathmandu Airport exit door, you will be transferred to hotel. Later visit Pashupatinath Temple, a sacred Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. Evening free for leisure. Overnight at the hotel in Kathmandu.

Day 02: Kathmandu – Chitwan National Park

After breakfast, transfer to Chitwan National Park, famous for its diverse flora and fauna. On arrival, check-in to the lodge/resort. Rest of the day free for leisure. Overnight stay in Chitwan.

Day 03: Chitwan National Park Sightseeing

Early morning Elephant Safari followed by Dugout Canoe Ride and visit to the Elephant Breeding Center. Later, enjoy a Village Walk in a Tharu village. In the evening, witness the "Tharu Tribal Dance." Overnight stay in Chitwan.

Day 04: Chitwan National Park - Pokhara

After breakfast, transfer to Pokhara, a beautiful city known for its lakes and mountain views. On arrival, check-in at the hotel. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay in Pokhara.

Day 05: Pokhara Sightseeing

Early morning visit to Sarangkot for sunrise views. Later, explore Vindyavasini Temple, David's Fall, Gupteshwar Mahadev Caves, and enjoy a boat ride on Phewa Lake (optional). Overnight stay in Pokhara.

Day 06: Pokhara - Kathmandu

Return to Kathmandu with an optional visit to Manakamana Temple via cable car (direct payment basis). Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.

Day 07: Kathmandu Sightseeing - Tribhuvan International Airport

After breakfast, visit Kathmandu Durbar Square, Swayambhunath Stupa, and later transfer to the airport for your return journey.

Note: International departures require check-in at least 3 hours before the flight.

  • Stay on Double Sharing basis
  • 05 Buffet Breakfast

  • English Speaking Driver Cum Guide
  • Entrance fee to all sites based on itinerary
  • Airport Pick up & Drop

Package Excludes:

  • Visa on arrival
  • Expenses of personal nature, mini bar in the room, laundry, telephone bills, tips, gratuities, etc.
  • Tips & Porterage for Local driver & guide
  • Cost of any other services not specifically mentioned in inclusions
