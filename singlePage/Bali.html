Bali Tours

4 Nights and 5 Days

Day 1: Airport Pick Up + Free and Easy

First day in program Bali Tour Packages 4 Nights and 5 Days Tours will start pick up at Airport. Our English Speaking Driver will Pick Up at Airport holding sign with your name, so will be easy for you to find us in airport, just looking sign with your name and our English Speaking Driver will be welcome you with friendly smile and we directly to your hotel for check in.

Note: Pick Up at Airport is based on your flight Timings

Day 2: Full day Barong and Kintamani Tour

After a buffet breakfast at the hotel, proceed towards Batubulan for a performance of the stunning Barong and Kris Dance. Later travel to the Bedulu Elephant Cave, in part a monastery dating back to the 9th Century, Gunung Kwai, an ancient tomb carved out of a huge rock face. From Tampaksiring the road climbs up to the Kintamani district, with its magnificent views of volcanic Mount and Lake Batur. After Enjoying Kintamani Volcanic Hill sightseeing return back to Hotel and Overnight at Hotel in Bali.

Day 3: Water Sports Activities and Evening Bali Hai Sunset Dinner Cruise

After Breakfast at the hotel, Proceed for the watersports activities at Tanjung Benoa beach. Enjoy The Exciting Bali Water Sports such as: Snorkeling, Parasailing, Jet Ski, Donut Boat, Water Ski, Flying Fish, Banana Boat, Wakeboarding, Ocean Walker, Diving. Glass Bottom Boat, and Turtle Islands Tour are included in Water sport.

Bali Sunset Dinner Cruise: Amazing Bali Cruise to enjoy sunset in cruise ship. As the sun goes down we set off on a relaxing cruise around the harbour. Take in the cool night air on the top deck, be entertained by our live musical entertainers at the bridge deck, savour a delicious international buffet dinner, then dance the night away at our disco or enjoy our live cabaret show. Bali Sunset Dinner Cruise is suitable for the whole family so bring the kids along to enjoy the party.

Day 4: Ubud Temple, Uluwatu and Tanah Lot Temple Visit

After breakfast proceed for Sightseeing. Visit Ubud Tegenungan Waterfall, one of the best waterfalls in Ubud village, offering an amazing scenery with clean water and cool atmosphere. Then visit Ubud Monkey Forest, a small rain forest dwelt by some group of monkeys and other tropical animals. After that, visit Tanah Lot Temple, a Balinese Hindu temple on the rock located in the middle of the sea. The word "Tanah Lot" consists of two words: "Tanah" interpreted as a reef looking like an isle, and "Lot" meaning sea. Evening return to Hotel and overnight stay.

Day 5: Free and Easy + Airport Transfer

Our English Speaking Driver will pick up you at hotel for the last day of The Bali Tour Packages 4 Nights and 5 Days Tours. We will pick up at hotel based on your flight details and the direction from the places you stay and then we directly to the airport for departure. Please check your stuff and belongings before we go to airport.

Note: International Departure requires 3 hours before check-in time at the airport.

  • Stay at Bali on Double Sharing basis
  • 05 Buffet Breakfast
  • All Sightseeing on PVT Basis
  • 120 Mins Spa Treatment at Bali Orchids Spa with transfers
  • One round of Banana Boat OR Parasailing
  • English Speaking Driver Cum Guide
  • Entrance fee to all sites based on itinerary
  • Airport Pick up & Drop
