Bandipur Safari

2 Nights and 3 Days

Day 1: Nagpur to Bandipur

The wild yet entertaining Bandipur National Park tour starts with your arrival in Nagpur from where your by road journey to Bandipur commences. The best advice is to reach your destination in the morning and commence the journey in the early hours of the day. By afternoon, you will reach the Bandipur National Park where your accommodation is booked in a good hotel or resort where you can check in and rest for some time. Enjoy a delicious lunch and then, in the evening visit attractions of the Bandipur National Park including wildlife safari. All of these attractions belong to ancient India and its remarkable history. After enjoying Bandipur sightseeing, delve into tasty dinner in a local restaurant and then back to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 2: Bandipur National Park

Get ready for experiencing the best of wildlife in Bandipur National Park as your safari is scheduled in the morning. First, have a hearty breakfast in the hotel and then, you will be taken to Bandipur National Park for safari. Spot endangered animals along with other rarely seen wildlife living in the park. After returning from Bandipur, enjoy lunch and rest for a while. In the evening, enjoy sightseeing at Bandipur resort and experience the freshness during nature walk. Dinner and overnight stay will be in the resort.

Day 3: Bandipur National Park and Return to Nagpur

On this day, enjoy early morning safari in the beautiful Bandipur National Park and spot the endangered animals, birds and other wild animals sheltering in the park. After the safari, back to the resort where you enjoy breakfast and then check out from there and drive back to Nagpur as here your tour reaches its concluding time. This tour offers a unique opportunity to experience the wild wonders of Bandipur National Park, providing a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.
