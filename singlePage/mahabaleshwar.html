Mahabaleshwar Tour Packages

Mahabaleshwar - Aamcha Mahabaleshwar

Day 1: PUNE TO MAHABALESHWAR

On arrival at Pune Airport/Station, you will depart in the afternoon from Pune to Mahabaleshwar on a private basis. After arriving at Mahabaleshwar, check in to the hotel. The rest of the day is free for your own leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel with dinner.

Day 2: MAHABALESHWAR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

After breakfast at the hotel, proceed for local sightseeing of Mahabaleshwar. Visit Old Mahabaleshwar, Panchganga, Arthur Seat Point, Hunting Point, Monkey Point, Panchgani Darshan, and other local attractions including waterfalls and strawberry gardens. Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: MAHABALESHWAR TO PUNE (DROP)

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed for the remaining sightseeing of Mahabaleshwar. Afterward, you will be dropped at Pune Station/Airport for your onward journey.

Includes:

  • Hotel accommodation in Standard Hotel on twin sharing DLX AC room
  • Meal Plan: CP (Room + Breakfast)
  • Sightseeing as per the itinerary in Sedan class vehicle
  • Pick up & Drop from Pune Airport / Railway Station / Bus Stand

Excludes:

  • Any Train Tickets / Air Tickets
  • Any Medical/rescue evacuation due to mishaps
  • Any entrance fee, Boating charges
  • Any personal nature items like Laundry, bar bills, table bills, camera fee, tips or any other item
  • Mineral water/Soft or hard drinks/Starters
  • Any other things that are not included in the "Package Cost Includes" Column
