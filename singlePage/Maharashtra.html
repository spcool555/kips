कोंकण - येवा कोंकण आपलोच आसा

4 Night 5 Day

Pune – Ganpati Pule – Ratnagiri – Sindhudurg – Tarkarli – Kolhapur

Day 1: PUNE TO GANPATI PULE

On Arrival at Pune Railway station / Airport, You will get pick up and Transfer to Ganpati Pule. After that Check in to hotel, have Dinner and stay overnight at the Hotel.

Day 2: GANPATI PULE – RATNAGIRI - SINDHUDURG

After breakfast proceed for local sightseeing visit Ratnadurga Fort, Bhagavatti Devi Mandir, Thiba Palace. After lunch proceed to Ganapatipule. Visit Ganesh Temple. After darshan proceed to Sindhudurg, Check in to Hotel and Have Overnight stay with Dinner.

Day 3: SINDHUDURG - TARKARLI LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

After Breakfast Checkout from hotel and proceed to Tarkarli. Day free for water sports activities at Devbagh beach. Visit Sangampoint & Tsunami point. You can enjoy Scuba Diving, Snorkeling & other Boat Rides. Evening free at Beach. Overnight stay at Tarkarli.

Day 4: TARKARLI TO KOLHAPUR

After Breakfast check out from hotel and proceed to Kolhapur. On arrival at Kolhapur Visit Mahalaxmi temple and Other Sightseeing. Check in to Hotel and Have Overnight stay with Dinner.

Day 5: KOLHAPUR – PUNE DROP

After Breakfast Checkout from hotel and you will get timely drop at Pune till evening.

Mumbai - City Of dreams

2 Nights & 3 Days

Day 1: Mumbai Arrival – Local Sightseeing

On Arrival at Mumbai Airport / Station you will get transfer to Hotel. After that, proceed for local sightseeing of Mumbai. Mumbai City Tour includes Gateway of India, Assembly Hall, Secretariat, Nariman Point, Marine Drive, Hanging Garden, Kamla Nehru Park, Mahalaxmi Temple, Mani Bhavan, Chowpatty Beach, Taraporwalla Aquarium, World Trade Centre, Jehangir Art Gallery, Prince of Wales Museum, Haji Ali, Bandra-Worli Sea Link, Race Course, Nehru Planetarium, Juhu Beach, Isckon Temple, etc. In the evening, return to the Hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2: Bollywood Bus Tour (2 Hrs) - Optional Tour

After Breakfast at Hotel, proceed for the sightseeing of Bollywood Film City. Visit the famous Film City in Goregaon. Visit shooting spots such as helipad, temple, church, lake, garden, court, etc. Visit a shooting site if possible and watch it from a bus. Visit a serial shooting set and enjoy a 20-minute video clip on Bollywood. Visit a dubbing studio and a live studio set of a serial. See behind-the-scenes activities of serial shooting, meet technicians at work, and if possible, photograph with serial stars. Visit the makeup rooms (if possible). In the evening, return to Hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Mumbai Airport / Station (Drop)

After Breakfast, checkout from the hotel and proceed for remaining sightseeing of Mumbai or visit famous shopping spots of Mumbai like Bandra Market, Link Road, Kharghar, Dadar, etc. After that, you will get a timely drop at Mumbai Station / Airport.

Mahabaleshwar - Aamcha Mahabaleshwar

2 Nights & 3 Days

Day 1: PUNE TO MAHABALESHWAR

On arrival at Pune Airport/Station, you will depart in the afternoon from Pune to Mahabaleshwar on a private basis. After arriving at Mahabaleshwar, check in to the hotel. The rest of the day is free for your own leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel with dinner.

Day 2: MAHABALESHWAR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

After breakfast at the hotel, proceed for local sightseeing of Mahabaleshwar. Visit Old Mahabaleshwar, Panchganga, Arthur Seat Point, Hunting Point, Monkey Point, Panchgani Darshan, and other local attractions including waterfalls and strawberry gardens. Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: MAHABALESHWAR TO PUNE (DROP)

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed for the remaining sightseeing of Mahabaleshwar. Afterward, you will be dropped at Pune Station/Airport for your onward journey.

Pachmarhi - Feel the nature

2 Nights & 3 Days

Day 1: Nagpur - Pachmarhi

Arrive at Pachmarhi. After arrival, proceed for sightseeing of Jatashankar, Handi Khoh, Priyadarshini, Mahadeo, Gupt Mahadeo, Pandav Gufa, and Rajendra Giri by Tavera. You will return to the hotel and have an overnight stay in Pachmarhi.

Day 2: Pachmarhi

Start your day with breakfast, then proceed for sightseeing to Apsara Vihar, Rajat Parbat, Panchali Kund. After lunch, continue sightseeing at Beefal and Dhoopgarh by open gypsy with a guide. Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay at Pachmarhi.

Day 3: Pachmarhi - Nagpur

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed for Nagpur by road. Tour ends with happy memories.
