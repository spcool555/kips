Bhutan Tours

5 Nights and 6 Days

Tour Itinerary for Railway Transfer

Day 1: Hasimara To Phuntsholing

Arrive at Hasimara Station. Meet our representative and transfer to Phuentsholing. Check into your hotel and relax. Overnight stay in Phuentsholing.

Day 2: Phuntsholing To Thimphu

After obtaining permits, drive to Thimphu, Bhutan's capital. En route, visit Kharbandi Ghumpha, a Buddhist monastery offering scenic views of Phuentsholing and Jaigaon. Check into your hotel and enjoy free time. Overnight stay in Thimphu.

Day 3: Full Day Thimphu Tour

Explore Thimphu with visits to Buddha Point, Folk Heritage Museum, Tashichho Dzong, BBS Tower, National Takin Zoo, and National Memorial Chorten. Overnight stay in Thimphu.

Day 4: Thimphu To Paro

Drive to Paro, another scenic town in Bhutan. Enjoy shopping in the local market. Overnight stay in Paro.
