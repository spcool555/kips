03 Nights Volvo Package Himachal

Day 1: Delhi To Manali – Board A/C Volvo Bus from Delhi at 5:30 PM. Manali is a picturesque hill station in Himachal Pradesh, known for its stunning natural beauty and scenic spots. The lofty Himalayas, pleasant sunshine, and emerald green surroundings add charm to Manali. Overnight journey in A/C Volvo Bus.

Day 2: Manali – Upon arrival in Manali, our representative will assist you to your pre-booked hotel. Check-in, freshen up, and enjoy leisure time. Afterward, enjoy a city tour visiting prominent attractions including Hadimba Devi Temple (Dhungri Temple), Manu Temple, Tibetan Monastery, and Vashisth Village. Return to the hotel in the afternoon for leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Manali.

Day 3: Manali – Solang Valley – Enjoy breakfast at the hotel and get ready for an excursion to Solang Valley. Known for its outstanding beauty, Solang Valley offers views of snow-covered mountains, serene natural surroundings, pristine lakes, and white snow throughout the year. After sightseeing, return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Manali – Kullu and Manikaran – After breakfast, depart for an excursion to Manikaran, known for the World’s hottest Sulphur Spring Waters and its significance as a holy place for Hindus and Sikhs. On the way back, visit the famous Kullu Shawl Industry. Return to Manali, check in at the hotel, and enjoy an overnight stay.

Day 5: Manali to Delhi – After breakfast, check out from the hotel at noon (luggage can be kept at reception). Enjoy leisure time or take a stroll down Mall Road in Manali to shop for antiques, shawls, and handicrafts. In the evening, catch the Volvo bus from Manali at 4:00 PM to Delhi. Overnight journey.

Day 6: Delhi – Arrival in Delhi, marking the end of the glorious tour to the heavenly Manali, offering you unforgettable memories to cherish forever.

2 Nights Shimla & 3 Nights Manali

Day 1: Delhi – Shimla (350 Km / 8 Hrs) – Upon arrival at Delhi Airport/Station, you will be transferred to Shimla by private car. You will reach Shimla by evening. Check-in to the hotel and enjoy an overnight stay with dinner.

Day 2: Shimla (Kufri) – After breakfast, drive to visit Kufri, Green Valley, Mini Zoo, Chini Bunglow, White Flower Hall, Halipad, Bhimakali Temple, and Indira Gandhi Tourist Park. In the evening, stroll through the famous shopping places of Shimla town – The Mall & the Ridge. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay with dinner.

Day 3: Shimla – Manali (250 Km / 8 Hrs) – After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Manali. You will reach Manali by evening. Check-in to the hotel and enjoy an overnight stay with dinner.

Day 4: Manali – After breakfast at the hotel, proceed for a half-day excursion to Solang Valley. You can opt for adventure activities like paragliding and snow trekking. Afterward, return to Manali and visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, Vashisht Village, Vashisht Hot Water Springs, Van Vihar, Hongkong Market, The Mall, and Tibetan Monastery. Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5: Manali – Kullu – Manikaran – After breakfast, proceed for an excursion to Kullu Valley and Manikaran. Enjoy rafting in the Beas River, and enroute, visit Kullu Market, Kullu Shawl factories, Angora Farm, Vaishno Mata Temple, and Parvati River. Return to Manali and explore the town on your own. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6: Manali – Delhi – After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Delhi by private car. You will reach Delhi by night. Timely drop at Delhi Airport/Station.
