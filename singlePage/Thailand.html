3N Phuket + 2N Bangkok

Day 1: Bangkok to Phuket
Arrive at Phuket Airport. You will be met by our representative and transferred to your hotel. After check-in, enjoy leisure time. Overnight stay in Phuket.

Day 2: Phuket (Phi Phi Island Tour)
After breakfast, proceed to explore the beautiful beaches and visit Phi Phi Island with lunch. This tour is by speedboat and includes snorkeling at Maya Bay, Loh Samah Bay, and Monkey Beach. Return and overnight stay in Phuket.

Day 3: Phuket (James Bond Island Tour)
After a hearty breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Phang Nga Bay for the James Bond Island tour. Enjoy the limestone cliffs and serene emerald-green waters. Lunch in the bay area. Evening at leisure. Return for dinner and overnight stay in Phuket.

Day 4: Phuket – Bangkok
After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Phuket Airport to board your flight to Bangkok. Upon arrival, transfer to hotel and check-in. Later, enjoy a half-day Bangkok city tour and explore shopping areas like Pratunam and MBK. Return for dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Bangkok (Safari World & Marine Park)
Breakfast at hotel. Then proceed for a full-day tour of Safari World and Marine Park with local lunch. See thrilling wildlife close-up and enjoy exciting shows including Dolphin, Sea Lion, Bird, and Cowboy Stunt shows. Evening return to hotel. Dinner on your own. Overnight stay in Bangkok.

Day 6: Bangkok (Airport Drop)
After breakfast, check out from hotel. You will be dropped at the airport as per your flight schedule.

Package Inclusion:

  • 03 Nights Accommodation in Phuket
  • 02 Nights Accommodation in Bangkok
  • Daily American / Continental Buffet Breakfast
  • James Bond Island Tour with Local Lunch
  • Phi Phi Island Tour with Local Lunch
  • Safari World & Marine Park with Local Lunch
  • Bangkok Half Day City Tour
  • All Transfers on SIC Basis

Package Excludes:

  • Airfare / Visa Fees
  • Meals other than those specified in the inclusions
  • Personal expenses like laundry, mini bar, etc.
  • Adventure Activity Charges
