Kashmir

5 Nights 6 Days Package

Day 1: Arrival Srinagar – Upon your arrival at the airport, meet our representative awaiting just outside the arrival hall, past the customs area and currency shops. In the afternoon, check into your pre-booked hotel. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Srinagar - Gulmarg – Srinagar – After breakfast, leave for Gulmarg (Meadow of Flowers). Situated 2730 meters above sea level, this gorgeous hamlet is especially beautiful in winter when covered in snow. Gulmarg boasts one of the best ski slopes in the world and the highest golf course with 18 holes. Under favorable weather conditions, arrangements for skiing and golf can be made on demand. If the weather permits, enjoy views of Nanga Parbat, the naked mountain over 26,000 ft that dominates the region. Other leisure activities such as a cable car (Gondola) ride up to Khilangmarg can also be arranged. Return to Srinagar for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 3: Srinagar - Sonmarg – Srinagar – Enjoy a full-day excursion to Sonmarg, known for its stunning drive from Srinagar. Stop at beautiful spots along the way. Continue to Sonmarg (2690 meters), where you can ride a horse to visit Thajiwas Glacier, which has snow year-round. Sonmarg is also known as the Gateway to Ladakh. In the evening, return to the houseboat for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Srinagar - Pahalgam – After breakfast, drive to Pahalgam (The Valley of Shepherds). En-route, visit the saffron fields of Pampore, renowned for producing the world's best saffron. Take a halt at Awantipura Ruins (1100 years old temple of Lord Vishnu built by King Awantivarman) and enjoy the beautiful countryside. Drive through pine forests to reach Pahalgam, where you can enjoy stunning views of the Lidder Valley. Take an evening walk along pony trails. Overnight stay at Pahalgam hotel.

Day 5: Pahalgam - Srinagar (Houseboat Stay) – After breakfast, embark on a sightseeing tour of the Mughal Gardens. Srinagar, with its placid lakes, enchanting surroundings, houseboats, and beautiful gardens, is truly heavenly. You will be directed to a deluxe houseboat for an overnight stay. Enjoy a traditional Shikara ride on Dal Lake, exploring Mughal Gardens like Shalimar, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi. In the evening, check into the houseboat for an overnight stay.

Day 6: Srinagar - Delhi Departure – Our representative will escort you to Srinagar Airport and make all arrangements for your convenient journey. Bid goodbye to the land of eternal beauty, heavenly charm, and the place most ideally called "the heaven on earth."
