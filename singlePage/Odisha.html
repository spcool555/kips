Odisha Package

02 Nights Puri + 1 Night Bhubaneswar

Day 01: Puri Local & Chilika - On arrival, check into the hotel. After breakfast, proceed to Chilika Lake. It is the largest wintering ground for migratory birds in the Indian sub-continent. Lunch will be provided during sightseeing. After returning to Puri, visit temples like Sonar Gourag Temple, Astasambhu Temple, Loknath Temple, Siddha Mahavir Temple, and Daksinakali Temple. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay at Puri.

Day 02: Puri – Konark - After breakfast, proceed to Konark. Visit Sun Temple (a world-famous heritage site, also known as Black Pagoda). Enroute, visit Ramchandi Temple and Chandrabhaga Beach (a beautiful beach known for its serene environment). Visit Puri Jagannath Temple and Gundicha Temple. Evening return to the hotel for an overnight stay with dinner.

Day 03: Puri – Bhubaneswar - After breakfast, check out from Puri hotel and proceed to Bhubaneswar. Visit Khandagiri & Udaigiri Jain Caves (the oldest rock-cut caves with traces of Buddhism and Jainism influence). After lunch, visit Nandankanan Zoo (closed on Monday), famous for white tiger safari and botanical garden. Overnight stay at Bhubaneswar.

Day 04: Bhubaneswar Drop - After breakfast, check out and either enjoy remaining sightseeing or get dropped at the Railway Station/Airport for your onward journey.

Includes:

  • Hotel accommodation in 3-star hotels
  • Meals as per choice
  • Sightseeing as per the itinerary
  • All Arrival/Departure transfer by A/C vehicle
