Hyderabad

02 Night 03 Days - Hyderabad

Day 01: HYDERABAD – On arrival at Hyderabad Railway Station/Airport, you will be greeted by our representative. Transfer to the hotel, freshen up, have breakfast, and proceed for a sightseeing tour of Hyderabad. Visit Salar Jung Museum, one of the three National Museums of India, followed by a visit to Birla Temple, Hussain Sagar Lake, and the Lumbini Park Laser Show. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 02: FULL DAY RAMOJI FILM CITY – After breakfast, proceed to Ramoji Film City (40 kms). Experience the world of film-making at the largest integrated film studio complex in the world, which spans over 1666 acres and houses hotels, restaurants, and guest rooms. Participate in organized tours of the premises. In the evening, return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 03: HYDERABAD CITY TOUR – After breakfast, continue with a sightseeing tour of the city. Visit Snow World, Charminar (located in the Old City), and Nehru Zoological Park. In the evening, get dropped off at the Railway Station/Airport.

Hyderabad with Sri Sailam

03 Night 04 Days - Hyderabad with Sri Sailam

Day 01: HYDERABAD CITY TOUR – On arrival at Hyderabad Railway Station/Airport, you will be greeted by our representative. Transfer to the hotel, freshen up, have breakfast, and proceed for a sightseeing tour of Hyderabad. Visit Salar Jung Museum, Birla Temple, Hussain Sagar Lake, and Lumbini Park Laser Show. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 02: HYDERABAD – SRI SAILAM DAY TOUR – After an early breakfast, begin your journey to Sri Sailam (4 hours travel). Visit Mallikarjun Temple and Sri Sailam Dam. Afterward, return to Hyderabad and check-in at the hotel for an overnight stay with dinner.

Day 03: RAMOJI FILM CITY – After breakfast, proceed to Ramoji Film City (40 kms). Explore the world’s largest integrated film studio complex, which spans 1666 acres. Take part in organized tours and enjoy the surroundings. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 04: HYDERABAD CITY TOUR – After breakfast, continue your sightseeing tour. Visit Snow World, Charminar, and Nehru Zoological Park. In the evening, get dropped off at the Railway Station/Airport.

  • All arrival/departure transfers by AC vehicle
