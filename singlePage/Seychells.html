Seychells Tours

5 Nights and 6 Days

Day 1: ARRIVE SEYCHELLES (MAHE AIRPORT)

As you arrive at the airport in Mahe, the tour agent’s representative will assist you in getting transferred to your hotel in Mahe. Upon arrival at the hotel in Mahe, check in and relax as the remaining part of the first day of your Seychelles tour is at leisure. A relaxing day ends with a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: REEF SAFARI (Optional)

After a delicious breakfast at the hotel, explore the Sainte Anne Marine Park on this exciting full-day excursion, jam-packed with activities. Set sail aboard Anahita as you head off to discover Mahé's inner islands. Hop aboard a semi-submersible vessel to explore the depths of the Marine Park. Enjoy a BBQ lunch and musical entertainment before heading ashore Moyenne Island. Visit the island, meet the Giant Tortoises, explore old ruins and pirate graves, or relax on the private beach. The day ends with a tropical cocktail and a leisurely cruise back to Mahe. Evening return to the hotel and overnight stay with dinner.

Day 3: MAHE DISCOVERY TOUR (Optional)

Experience the diversity and inspiring scenic beauty of Mahé, Seychelles' largest and most populated island. Begin with a tour of the capital, Victoria, and visit places of interest such as the Victoria Clock Tower and the colourful Sir Selwyn-Selwyn Clark Marketplace. Travel through Sans Souci, lush forests, and tea plantations along one of the most scenic routes in Seychelles. Stop at the historic Mission Lodge, enjoy a Creole Buffet in a local restaurant, visit a historic herb & spice garden, and explore a local village to experience Creole culture.

Day 4: ISLAND COMBO PRASLIN & LA DIGUE (Optional)

Join an unforgettable action-packed, full-day guided tour of two of Seychelles' most enchanting islands, Praslin and La Digue. Visit the prehistoric forest of Vallée de Mai and be mesmerised by its unique beauty before heading to the world-renowned Anse Lazio beach. Enjoy a delicious Créole lunch. On La Digue, visit the picturesque harbour and La Passe village, experience Créole architecture, historical sites such as L'Union Estate, the Copra Mill, and the famous beach, Anse Source d'Argent.

Day 5: A GOODBYE DAY

Savor one more breakfast at the hotel before completing check-out formalities. Spend the day at leisure on the beach. In the evening, admire the sight of Victoria and the surrounding islands. Later, enjoy leisure time before your transfer to the airport for your flight back home.

  • All Local Sightseeing and Transfers by SIC Basis (Optional)
  • All Taxes
  • Visa On Arrival
