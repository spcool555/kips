Day 2: Bollywood Bus Tour (2 Hrs) - Optional Tour

After Breakfast at Hotel, proceed for the sightseeing of Bollywood Film City. Visit the famous Film City in Goregaon. Visit shooting spots such as helipad, temple, church, lake, garden, court, etc. Visit a shooting site if possible and watch it from a bus. Visit a serial shooting set and enjoy a 20-minute video clip on Bollywood. Visit a dubbing studio and a live studio set of a serial. In the evening, return to Hotel for an overnight stay.
