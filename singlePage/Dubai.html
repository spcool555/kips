4 Nights and 5 Days Dubai

Day 1: Arrive Dubai + Dhow Cruise with Dinner

Welcome to Dubai! Upon arrival at Dubai Airport, you will be met outside the arrival hall and transferred to your hotel. In the evening, enjoy a romantic dinner aboard a traditional Arabic Dhow that glides along the Dubai Creek under the starry sky. Admire landmarks like the Dubai Creek Golf and Yacht Club and Sheikh Saeed’s house. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Dubai City Tour + Burj Khalifa

After a buffet breakfast, embark on a guided half-day city tour covering Dubai Creek, Jumeirah Mosque (Photo Stop), Burj Al Arab, Atlantis the Palm, Burj Khalifa, Jumeirah Beach, Madinat Jumeirah, and Sheikh Zayed Road. Visit ‘At The Top’ observation deck on the 124th floor of Burj Khalifa. Later, return to the hotel.

Day 3: Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner

After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely morning. In the afternoon, experience the Desert Safari with dune bashing, camel rides, henna designs, traditional Arabic attire, belly dancing, and a delicious BBQ dinner. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: Abu Dhabi City Tour + Ferrari World

After breakfast, embark on a tour of Abu Dhabi. Visit Sheikh Zayed Mosque, Women’s Handicraft Centre, Qasr Al-Hosn Palace, and Abu Dhabi Heritage Village. Later, explore Ferrari World, home to the world’s fastest roller coaster. Return to Dubai for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Depart Dubai

Enjoy breakfast before being transferred to the airport for your flight back home.

5 Nights and 6 Days With Atlantis Palm Ex. Dubai

Day 1: Arrive Dubai + Dhow Cruise with Dinner

Arrive in Dubai, transfer to the hotel, and enjoy a relaxing Dhow Cruise with Dinner in the evening. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner

After breakfast, enjoy a day at leisure before heading for a Desert Safari with dune bashing, camel rides, henna painting, belly dancing, and a BBQ dinner.

Day 3: Abu Dhabi City Tour + Ferrari World

Post breakfast, visit Abu Dhabi’s key attractions, including Sheikh Zayed Mosque, Heritage Village, and Ferrari World. Later, return to Dubai.

Day 4: Dubai City Tour + Burj Khalifa

Explore Dubai’s famous sites like Jumeirah Mosque, Burj Al Arab, Atlantis the Palm, Burj Khalifa, and Dubai Mall. Visit the ‘At The Top’ observation deck at Burj Khalifa.

Day 5: Atlantis The Palm

Check out from the hotel and transfer to Atlantis The Palm. Enjoy a fun-filled day at Aquaventure Water Park and Lost Chambers. Experience luxury at the Atlantis resort.

Day 6: Depart Dubai

After breakfast, enjoy leisure time before being transferred to the airport for departure.
