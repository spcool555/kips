Singapore Tours

4 Nights & 5 Days - Singapore

Day 1 – Singapore Arrival – Night Safari

On arrival in the cheerful ambiance of Singapore, you will be transferred to your chosen hotel. Check-in at 2:00 pm. After freshening up, enjoy your leisure time. In the evening, proceed for the 'Night Safari' tour, the world's premier night zoo. Experience a unique exploration of wilds in a tropical jungle at night. Enjoy dinner at an Indian restaurant and return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2 - Singapore City Tour & Sentosa Island

After breakfast, enjoy a panoramic half-day city tour. Visit landmarks like Padang Cricket Club, Parliament House, Supreme Court, City Hall, and Saint Andrew's Cathedral. Stop at the Thian Hock Keng Temple, the oldest temple in Singapore. Enjoy entry to the Flyer. Later, visit Sentosa Island, featuring a cable car ride, Wings of Time show, Luge & Sky Ride, and more. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3 - Universal Studios

After breakfast, head to Universal Studios, Southeast Asia's first Hollywood movie theme park. Experience thrilling rides and attractions based on your favorite movies. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay with dinner.

Day 4 - Gardens By the Bay

After breakfast, enjoy a free day for shopping. In the evening, visit the award-winning Gardens by the Bay and enter the Cloud Forest, featuring a 35-meter tall mountain and one of the world's tallest indoor waterfalls. At night, enjoy the mesmerizing light and sound show at the Super Trees. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 5 - Singapore Airport Drop (Late Night)

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and enjoy a free day for leisure. Later at night, transfer to the airport for your flight back home.

Package Inclusion:

  • 04 Nights Accommodation in Singapore
  • Daily Breakfast at Hotel
  • Half-Day City Tour in Singapore with Flyer
  • Sentosa Extreme with Cable Car, Wings of Time, Luge Ride, Madame Tussauds, Maritime Museum
  • Universal Studios / Gardens By The Bay
  • Return Airport Transfers on SIC Basis
  • Single Entry Singapore Visa
  • All Tours and Transfers on SIC Basis

Package Excludes:

  • Airfare
  • Any other expenses not mentioned in the inclusions

Singapore with Cruise - 3 Nights Singapore + 2 Nights Cruise

Day 1 – Singapore Arrival – Night Safari

On arrival in Singapore, transfer to your hotel and check-in at 2:00 pm. In the evening, enjoy the 'Night Safari' tour. Witness exotic animals under subtle lighting in a tropical jungle. Enjoy dinner at an Indian restaurant and return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2 - Singapore City Tour & Sentosa Island

After breakfast, take a half-day city tour covering key landmarks. Visit Thian Hock Keng Temple and enjoy the Singapore Flyer. Later, visit Sentosa Island for an adventure-filled evening with a cable car ride, Wings of Time show, and Luge Ride. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3 - Universal Studios

Enjoy a full day at Universal Studios. Experience world-class attractions, thrilling rides, and shows based on blockbuster movies. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay with dinner.

Day 4 - Singapore Cruise Transfer

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to the cruise. Check-in and enjoy a luxurious overnight stay on the cruise.

Day 5 – Full Day at Cruise

Relax and enjoy a full day at sea on the cruise.

Day 6 – Cruise Check-Out – Transfer to Airport

After breakfast, check out from the cruise and transfer to Singapore Airport for your departure flight.
