Rajasthan Package

Jaipur – Ajmer – Pushkar – Chittorgarh – Udaipur – Mt. Abu

Day 1: JAIPUR

On Arrival at Jaipur Railway station / Airport. Get Pick up and Transfer to Pre Booked Hotel. After breakfast, start for a full day local sightseeing in Jaipur. Visit Amber Fort & Palace, "Gaitore" Cenotaphs (Front View), take a photo stop at Jal Mahal, City Palace & Museum, Jantar Mantar (Observatory) and take a photo stop at Hawa Mahal. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay at Jaipur.

Day 2: JAIPUR – AJMER – PUSHKAR

Check out from Hotel & you will be driven to Ajmer and visit the famous Sufi Sant Moinuddin Chisti Dargah. Thereafter, depart to Pushkar. Visit Brahma Temple, Man Mahal, Pushkar Palace, and Pushkar Lake. Overnight stay at Pushkar.

Day 3: PUSHKAR – CHITTORGARH – UDAIPUR

After Breakfast, check out from hotel and transfer to Udaipur city. Enroute visit to Chittorgarh Fort. Chittorgarh is famously known for Rani Padmavati. Visit Chittorgarh Fort, the biggest fort of India. After that, drive to Udaipur. On arrival at Udaipur, check-in to Hotel and Overnight stay.

Day 4: UDAIPUR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

This morning, after breakfast, we proceed for local sightseeing in Udaipur city. Visit the City Palace, the biggest palace in India. Later, visit Lake Pichola, Saheli Ki Bari, and in the evening, roam around the market of Udaipur. Later, return to the hotel and have an overnight stay.

Day 5: UDAIPUR – MT ABU (DROP)

Check out from the hotel & today we transfer to Mount Abu. On reaching Mount Abu, visit Nakki Lake, Pandav Bhavan, Dilwara Jain Temple, Brahmakumari Center, and Sunset Point. After that, you will be dropped at the Railway station.
