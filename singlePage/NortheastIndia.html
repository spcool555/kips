03 Nights Honeymoon Volvo Package - North East

Day 1: Delhi To Guwahati – Board A/C Volvo Bus from Delhi at 5:30 PM. Guwahati is a picturesque city in Assam, known for its stunning natural beauty and scenic spots. The mighty Brahmaputra River, pleasant sunshine, and emerald green surroundings add charm to Guwahati. Overnight journey in A/C Volvo Bus.

Day 2: Guwahati – Upon arrival in Guwahati, our representative will assist you to your pre-booked hotel. Check-in, freshen up, and enjoy leisure time. Afterward, enjoy a city tour visiting prominent attractions including Kamakhya Temple, Umananda Temple, and Assam State Museum. Return to the hotel in the afternoon for leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Guwahati.

Day 3: Guwahati – Shillong – Enjoy breakfast at the hotel and get ready for an excursion to Shillong. Known for its outstanding beauty, Shillong offers views of snow-covered mountains, serene natural surroundings, pristine lakes, and white snow throughout the year. After sightseeing, return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Shillong – Cherrapunji – After breakfast, depart for an excursion to Cherrapunji, known for the World’s highest rainfall and its significance as a holy place for Hindus and Sikhs. On the way back, visit the famous Elephant Falls. Return to Shillong, check in at the hotel, and enjoy an overnight stay.

Day 5: Shillong to Guwahati – After breakfast, check out from the hotel at noon (luggage can be kept at reception). Enjoy leisure time or take a stroll down Police Bazaar in Shillong to shop for antiques, shawls, and handicrafts. In the evening, catch the Volvo bus from Shillong at 4:00 PM to Guwahati. Overnight journey.

Day 6: Guwahati – Arrival in Guwahati, marking the end of the glorious tour to the heavenly North East, offering you unforgettable memories to cherish forever.
