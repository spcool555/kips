Agra – Mathura – Vrindavan

Agra – Mathura – Vrindavan 2 Nights & 3 Days

Day 1: MATHURA - VRINDAVAN LOCAL SIGHTSEEING: On Arrival at Mathura Junction, you will get picked up and transferred to the hotel. After breakfast, proceed for Mathura – Vrindavan sightseeing. Mathura is famous as the birthplace of Lord Krishna. Visit the Sri Krishna Janmabhoomi Temple complex, Ghats, and the famous Gita Mandir. Later, visit Vrindavan, known as Krishna’s childhood home. Explore Govind Dev and Rangaji temples. Return to Mathura in the evening and stay overnight at the hotel.

Day 2: MATHURA – BARSANA - GOWARDHAN HILL After breakfast, proceed to Barsana, famous for the Radha Rani Temple. Then, visit Govardhan Hill, located 22 km from Mathura. This sandstone hill stands 80 ft tall and has a 38 km circumference. Key attractions include Mansi Ganga, Mukharvind, and Daan Ghati. Devotees often perform Govardhan Parikrama by walking 23 km barefoot around the hill. Return to Mathura in the evening and stay overnight at the hotel.

Day 3: AGRA LOCAL SIGHTSEEING & DROP After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed for Agra sightseeing. Visit the Taj Mahal, Red Fort, and local markets. In the evening, after dinner, you will get a timely drop at the railway station.
