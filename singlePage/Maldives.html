Maldives Tours

4 Nights and 5 Days

Day 1: ARRIVE MALDIVES

Sun, sand, and sea, a thousand 'Robinson Crusoe' islands, massive lagoons with different depths and infinite shades of blue and turquoise, dazzling underwater coral gardens; a perfect natural combination for the ideal tropical holiday destination. On arrival, you will be transferred to your resort by water taxi / Speedboat / Sea Plane. Overnight at your resort.

Day 2: LEISURE & WATER ACTIVITIES

Enjoy breakfast. The day is free to explore your island on your own. Go swimming, scuba diving, snorkeling, fishing, and enjoy a day at leisure. Overnight at your resort hotel.

Day 3: RELAXATION & MASSAGE

Enjoy breakfast. The day is free to relax on the white sandy beach or indulge in a luxurious massage. Overnight at your resort hotel.

Day 4: FULL DAY AT LEISURE

Enjoy a full day at leisure, exploring the beauty of the Maldives at your own pace. Overnight at your resort.

Day 5: DEPART MALDIVES

After breakfast, depart for the airport by water taxi / speedboat / sea plane for your flight back home or onward destination.

Package Inclusion:

  • Accommodation as mentioned above.
  • Meals (Breakfast + Lunch + Dinner)
  • Assistance at the airport by hotel representatives
  • Welcome drinks on arrival
  • A Bottle of Water 500ml per person per day in the room
  • Tea / Coffee making facilities (1 set per room per day)
  • Free Wi-Fi in all rooms
  • Gymnasium (excluding personal trainer)
  • Land sports including Badminton, Snooker/Billiards, Table Tennis, Beach Volleyball, Squash, Sauna, Steam bath, Tennis, Basketball, Jacuzzi (excluding coach and instructors)
  • Windsurfing and canoeing
  • Free drinking water at meal times served by glass
  • A Bottle of Sparkling Wine and a Fruit Basket for Honeymooners (for weddings not older than 6 months)
  • Combined Day Time Speed Boat Airport Transfers
  • All current Maldivian Taxes included

Package Excludes:

  • Any item of personal nature like porterage & tips, laundry, telephone calls, beverages, mineral water, etc.
  • Any services or meals other than the ones mentioned above.
  • Any other expenses not mentioned in the program or 'cost includes'.
