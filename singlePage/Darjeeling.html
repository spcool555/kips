Darjeeling Tour Packages

Darjeeling - "India's Own Moonland"

A land like no other with superabundance of attractions to visit and phantasmagoric and fabulous landscapes, amazing people and culture, Ladakh is truly a heaven on Earth.Bounded by two of the world's mightiest mountain ranges, the Great Himalaya and the Karakoram, it lies athwart two other, the Ladakh range and the Zanskar range. Ladakh is mystical in all the spheres it covers, from nature, geography, sceneries to the modest cultures that it fosters. Right from gompas to the sensational momos, the superabundance of attractions to visit makes this city make it heaven on earth. It is said that only in Ladakh can a man sitting in the sun with his feet in the shade suffer from sunstroke and frostbite at the same time.

North-East Package - 2 Nights Darjeeling + 2 Nights Gangtok

Day 1: NJP/Bagdogra – Gangtok (4 – 4.5 hrs) – Meet & greet with our representative at Bagdogra Airport & transfer to Gangtok. On arrival, check in to the hotel. In the evening, visit the local market or nearby areas (at your own). Overnight at hotel.

Day 2: Full day Lake & Baba Mandir Excursion Trip (Nathula Optional, Depend on Permit) – After breakfast, drive to Tsomgo Lake (12000ft), a high-altitude alpine lake close to the Indo-China Border. Enjoy playing in the snow and try Yak Ride, then proceed to Baba Harbhajan Singh Mandir. Return to hotel. Night stay at Gangtok hotel. Optional tour to Nathula Pass (14000 ft) for Indians with 30 days advance request. Overnight stay at Gangtok.

Day 3: Gangtok – Darjeeling – After breakfast, transfer to Darjeeling. Check into the hotel. In the evening, you can visit the local market or nearby areas on your own. Overnight at Darjeeling.

Day 4: Darjeeling Sightseeing – Early Morning visit to Tiger Hill to watch the spectacular sunrise over Mt. Khangchendzongha (28,208 ft), followed by visits to Ghoom Monastery, Batasia Loop, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, P. N. Zoological Park, Tenzing Rock, Tibetan Refugee self-help Centre, Tea Garden, Peace Pagoda, and Japanese Temple. Overnight at Darjeeling.

Day 5: Departure from Darjeeling + Toy Train – After breakfast, enjoy a Himalayan Heritage Joy Ride (additional cost), then have leisure time for packing and personal activities. Transfer to Bagdogra Airport/NJP Railway Station for your onward journey.

North-East Package - 2 Nights Darjeeling + 1 Night Kalimpong + 2 Nights Gangtok

Day 1: NJP Rly Station/IXB Airport – Darjeeling (75 kms/3 hrs) – Meet & Greet at NJP Railway Station/IXB Airport & transfer to Darjeeling (6,950 ft.). On arrival, check into hotel and rest of the day is at leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 2: Darjeeling Sightseeing – Early Morning drive to Tiger Hill to watch sunrise over Mt. Khangchendzongha (28,208 ft), visit Ghoom Monastery, Batasia Loop, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, P.N. Zoological Park, Tenzing Rock, Tibetan Refugee self-help Centre, Tea Garden, Ropeway, and Japanese Temple. Evening free for shopping. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 3: Darjeeling – Kalimpong (57 kms/2.5 hrs) – After breakfast, transfer to Kalimpong. Afternoon visit Mangal Dham, Deolo Hill, Dr. Graham’s Home, Golf Garden, Durpin Dara Hills, and Pine View Nursery. Overnight stay at Kalimpong.

Day 4: Kalimpong – Gangtok (75 kms/3 hrs) Local Sightseeing – After breakfast, transfer to Gangtok. Afternoon city tour including Flower Show, Cottage Industry, Handicraft Centre, Institute of Tibetology, Dro-dul Chorten, Enchey Monastery, and Banjakhri Falls. Overnight stay at Gangtok.

Day 5: Excursion to Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir – After breakfast, start for Tsomgo Lake (12,400 ft.) & Baba Mandir (13,200 ft.). Return to hotel in the evening. Overnight stay at Gangtok.

Day 6: Gangtok – NJP Rly Station/IXB Airport – After breakfast, check-out from hotel and transfer to NJP Railway Station/IXB Airport for your onward journey.
