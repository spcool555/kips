1 Nights and 2 Days

Gir Fun Tour with 1 Jeep Safari

1 Nights and 2 Days

Day 1: Arrive Sasan Gir – Transfer To Wildlife Resort

Our services starts after you arrive at Gir National Park. You will be received by our office executive and transfer to wildlife resort. On reaching, complete the check-in formalities at your pre-booked jungle lodge. Take meals and have rest for few hours. The evening can be spent as per your wish. Enjoy dinner and overnight in Gir. Be prepared to be greeted with different species of birds next morning chirping outside your verandah.

Day 2: Gir National Park

Start your day with the buffet of breakfast at the resort and get ready to take a plunge into the natural diversity i.e. full of wild richness. Today Morning is dedicated to wild Safari in the Gir National Park. During the open jeep safari, you can hope to see various animal species such as Sambar, Wild Boar, Chital, Nilgai, Chinkara, and Four-horned Antelope and many more. The three hours game drive moves amidst the deciduous forest – the last abode of the Asiatic lion and also has thriving panther population. A number of attractive birds can usually be seen on the trail too. After you finish safari come back to resort and take refreshment, check out from resort on time and transfer to onward destination.

Tour and Services End.
