Ladakh Package

Ladakh Package - 5 Nights 6 Days

Day 1: Arrive in Leh – On arrival at Leh, Kips Tourism Representative will greet and assist you to your hotel. The rest of the day is free for acclimatization to the high altitudes of Ladakh. In the evening, take a walk and embrace the nature around you. Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 2: Leh Local Sightseeing – After breakfast, visit the hot spots of Leh. You’ll be awestruck by the sight of the Indus & Zanskar River Sangam, and visit Pathar Sahib Gurudwara located 25 miles away. Drive to Magnetic Hill, where the surrounding slopes create an appearance of a downhill road, and then visit the Hall of Fame. Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 3: Leh - Nubra Valley (160 Km) – Travel to Diskit, the headquarters of Nubra Valley, and visit the ancient Diskit Monastery built in the 14th century. Continue to Hunder, famous for its sand dunes and the extraordinary Bactrian Camel, also known as the “Two Humped Camel.” After a memorable day in Nubra Valley, enjoy dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 4: Nubra Valley - Pangong (274 Km) – After an early breakfast, leave for Pangong Lake, a popular tourist attraction. The lake, located on the Indo-China border, is surrounded by quaint villages. Arrive at the lake before sunset to soak in the breathtaking views. Enjoy dinner and stay overnight at the campsite at Pangong.

Day 5: Pangong - Leh (225 Km) – After breakfast, check out from the campsite and return to Leh. Upon arrival, explore the local village and shop for pashmina shawls and handicrafts. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel in Leh.

Day 6: Departure – After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to the airport for your onward journey. Depart with memories galore!

Ladakh Package - 7 Nights 8 Days

Ladakh Package - 7 Nights 8 Days

Day 1: Arrive in Leh – On arrival at Leh, Kips Tourism Representative will greet and assist you to your hotel. The rest of the day is free for acclimatization to the high altitudes of Ladakh. In the evening, take a walk and embrace the nature around you. Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 2: Leh Local Sightseeing – After breakfast, visit the hot spots of Leh, including the Indus & Zanskar River Sangam, Pathar Sahib Gurudwara, Magnetic Hill, and Hall of Fame. Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 3: Leh - Nubra Valley (160 Km) – Travel to Diskit, the headquarters of Nubra Valley, known for its ancient monastery. Continue to Hunder, famous for its sand dunes and Bactrian Camel. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay in Nubra Valley.

Day 4: Hunder - Turtuk - Nubra – After breakfast, visit Turtuk, a small village on the banks of the Shyok River with unparalleled natural beauty. Spend your time walking around and enjoying the picturesque views. Return to Nubra for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 5: Nubra Valley - Leh (160 Km) – After breakfast, return to Leh. Check in to your hotel and spend the day at leisure. Explore nearby shops and sights. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay in Leh.

Day 6: Leh - Pangong (225 Km) – After an early breakfast, leave for Pangong Lake, a popular tourist attraction that has gained further popularity as a “hot-spot” for many film shoots. The lake, located on the Indo-China border, is surrounded by quaint villages of Shey and Gompa. We would reach the lake a little before sunset, where you can soak in the breathtaking views of the crystal clear water and majestic mountains. After a relaxed evening, enjoy dinner and stay overnight at the campsite at Pangong.

Day 7: Pangong - Leh (223 Km) – After breakfast, check out and drive back to Leh. You can visit the village and wander around to enjoy the beauty that Leh exhibits. The last night will be completed with dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel in Leh.

Day 8: Departure – With a heavy heart, your journey to mother nature “Leh” ends here. After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to the airport for your onward journey. Depart with memories galore!
